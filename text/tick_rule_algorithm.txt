Tick Test: When a trade occurs exactly at the midpoint, the Quote Rule is indeterminate. 
In this case, the Tick Test is applied. 
It compares the current trade price to the price of the previous trade (Pt−1​). 
If the current price is higher (an uptick), the trade is classified as a buy. 
If it is lower (a downtick), it is a sell. If the price is unchanged, the classification carries over from the previous trade.
